Brigade Eternia – Where Life Finds Its Forever Address.

Spread across a sprawling 14 acres of prime real estate in Yelahanka, North Bengaluru, Brigade Eternia is a pre-launch ultra-luxury gated community featuring 12 thoughtfully designed towers with a 2B + G + 13/14 structure. Designed for discerning homebuyers, this low-density development houses only 1124 premium residences, ensuring that over 75% of the total land area is dedicated to lush green open spaces, landscaped parks, and world-class amenities. Every home is a masterpiece of modern architecture, carefully crafted to maximize natural light, cross-ventilation, and privacy.
